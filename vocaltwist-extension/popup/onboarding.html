Getting started

1 Click into any text field on any website.
2 A mic button 🎙 appears near the field — click it or press Ctrl+Shift+V.
3 Speak. Your words are transcribed and inserted automatically.
4 Optional: Run VocalTwist locally for Whisper STT + Neural TTS. The extension auto-upgrades.

🔒 Browser mode uses Google's speech servers. Run VocalTwist locally for true privacy.
